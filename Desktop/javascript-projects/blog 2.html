vision

posted by Jay @ 7th Jan 2024

To create a nurturing and empowering environment where vulnerable, orphaned, and street children can flourish, discover their potential, and build a brighter future for themselves and their communities

mission

posted by Jay @ 7th Jan 2024

Evergreen Haven is dedicated to providing holistic care, education, and support to vulnerable children in Kenya. Our mission is to offer a safe haven where children can heal, learn, and grow into self-reliant individuals. Through a family-centered approach, we aim to empower them with life skills, education, and emotional well-being, enabling them to break free from the cycle of poverty and become responsible citizens

our inspiration

posted by Jay @ 7th Jan 2024

"Every child deserves a sanctuary, a place where they can grow, thrive, and find solace amidst the storms of life. Evergreen Haven emerges as a beacon of hope, a shelter that nurtures the hearts and minds of young souls. Inspired by the timeless beauty and resilience of nature, we strive to create an environment that fosters growth, healing, and endless possibilities. Just as evergreen trees stand tall and unyielding through all seasons, we stand unwaveringly by the side of each child, providing them a safe haven where their dreams can take flight. Together, we cultivate a future where every child's light shines bright, forever blooming in the lush landscapes of their imagination
